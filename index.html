3. Paid Campaigns — Condensed (Copy-ready)
Objectives
  • Lead generation: Google & Meta
  • Awareness: Instagram, Facebook, YouTube
  • Retargeting: Meta Pixel & Google Display
  • Consideration: WhatsApp, Email, LinkedIn
Google Ads
  • Use Search for high-intent keywords like "solar panel installation in Bangalore" and "get solar quote"
  • Budget ₹700–₹1,500/day initially; add call & location extensions
Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)
  • Target by city + interests (renewable energy, home improvement); ages 28–60
  • Use short videos, image ads and lead forms; local language captions help
LinkedIn (B2B)
  • Target facility heads, builders, and procurement roles for industrial & developer leads
